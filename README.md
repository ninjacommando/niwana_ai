# Niwana (නිවන) - The AI Spiritual Guide ☸️

**Niwana** is a state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence system designed to bridge the gap between ancient Buddhist philosophy and modern technology. It doesn't just provide answers; it trains the user's mind to perceive reality through the lens of the Dhamma.

---

## 🌟 The Vision
In a world spinning like a machine, stress and suffering have become the norm. **Niwana** is built to "wash away" mental pollution. Our goal is to provide a subconscious guidance system where any life problem is met with a path toward inner peace and liberation.

## ✨ Core Features
- **Dhamma-Logic Engine:** An AI trained on deep Buddhist scriptures to simulate the path of Nirvana.
- **Subconscious Guidance:** Helps users restructure their thinking patterns automatically when discussing life challenges.
- **Non-Commercial & Open Discovery:** A project born out of pure Dhamma exploration (Dharma Gaweshanaya).
- **Zero-Pressure Interface:** Designed for deep mental clarity and focus.

## 🛠 Tech Stack (Proposed)
- **Model:** fine-tuned LLM (Large Language Model) on Pali Canon and Dhamma translations.
- **Language:** Python / TypeScript.
- **Frameworks:** PyTorch / TensorFlow / LangChain.
- **Architecture:** Subconscious pattern recognition for mental well-being.

## 🧘 How It Works
> "It is not just about washing clothes; it is about washing the mind."

The AI interacts with the user's current life situation. Instead of giving generic advice, it uses a specialized training model to guide the user's mind toward detachment (*Viraga*) and peace (*Shanthi*), helping them realize the Dhamma through their own experiences.
